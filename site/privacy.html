Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 · Operated by Kredesolutions

Post Generator (“we”, “our”, “us”) is operated by Kredesolutions. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our content marketing platform at www.kredesolutions.com, api.kredesolutions.com, and the Post Generator application at kjsolutioncontentgenerator.streamlit.app.

Information we collect

  • Account data: email address, password (stored hashed), and company name.
  • Profile data: company and brand information you provide in the app.
  • Content: posts, marketing plans, uploaded files, and knowledge base entries.
  • Social connections: when you connect LinkedIn or Facebook, we receive OAuth tokens and basic profile or Page identifiers needed to publish on your behalf. We do not receive your social network password.
  • Usage: API and AI usage logs for billing, support, and security.

How we use information

  • Provide login, content generation, scheduling, and publishing features.
  • Publish posts to LinkedIn or Facebook only when you authorize us and trigger publishing.
  • Improve reliability, security, and product functionality.
  • Communicate with you about your account or service updates.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers required to operate the product (e.g. hosting, OpenAI for content generation, LinkedIn/Meta APIs for publishing). We do not sell your personal data.

Retention & security

We retain data while your account is active. OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest. You may disconnect social accounts or request account deletion by contacting us.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
